Literature Review

The dramatic aging of the world’s population underscores an urgent need for research to focus on the elder people . Fall detection is one of the orientation.

There are two main different ways to detect fall previously according to Raul: context-aware systems and wearable devices . The context-aware systems are operated by analysing the information provided by the sensor deployed previously, and the target person do not need to wear any special devices. And the wearable devices literally means the miniature sensor-based devices that can be worn by the target.

And the sensors of context-aware systems can be classified into normal video sensors, infrared sensors, microphones and pressure sensors. The wearable devices can be classified into accelerometer devices, smart phone embedded accelerometer. And the devices in smart phone has many sensors can be analysed: accelerometer, digital compass, gyroscope, GPS, microphone, and camera . The research of it nearly combined all of the functions above.

Normally, the method of context-aware systems started with background subtraction , and extract the objects from the detection by some features. And then, it discriminates falls from non-falls in various methods: coefficient based on the motion history , two-step fall model , multi-frame Gaussian classifier , hidden Markov models , etc.

But the methods above are incomplete yet: the algorithms are dependent on the placement of camera; the test data do not have representativeness which only test a few persons’ fallen; the use of video arouse people’s concern about privacy.

Features of fall

Falls are a major public health problem among older people. More falls take place in indoor environment and most of it happened in bedroom in America . Besides, the people alone has more chance to injure because of falls . And the main reason of it is no timely help after falling. According to these facts we could largely reduce the injures caused by falls by detect falls happened in bedroom which is an indoor environment when people are alone.

Falls can be classified into 6 types : slips, trips, extrinsic falls, intrinsic falls, falls from an upper level and other non-defined mechanisms. But compare with the situation analysed above, many types of falls can be filtered, and remains only two types of falls, including slips and traps. The common ground of it is the large move of height. The algorithm behind will make fully use of this facts.

Features of Kinect

Kinect has wide range of vertical field and horizontal field. Four data streams are provided by kinect: color stream, depth stream, sound stream and the infrared stream provided by the color image stream. There is only one color image stream available per sensor. The infrared image stream is provided by the color image stream.

The most valuable data kinect provided is the skeletal information, which is the result of machine learning training, and contains the positions of most useful joints. Although it is trained to recognize the user facing the sensor, but the result of evaluation shows that even not face to the sensor, the height of each joint is well recognized, and it is sufficient to detect fall as well. And it works well in the dark as well, which could meet the requirement that detect the fall through the day

Algorithm

Adjust Kinect Mode

First is to adjust the kinect mode according to the position and posture of people. The joints of head, heap center, two knee will be used in this step. The first part of adjust kinect mode is to get the position of the hip center to determing the mode should be near mode or default mode. The second part is to calculate the distance of hip center and knees to determine the mode should be seat mode or stand mode.

Detect Motion

The second step of fall detection is to detect the motion. In this step two data of 4 specific joints would be calculated to determine whether the motion is about to fall. The first part of this step is to get the average acceleration of each joint. The second part is to calculate the distance between the joint and the floor plane. In the third part, threshold of 0.05 and 0.8 is used to detect motion.

Judge Motion

The last step of fall detection is to judge whether the motion detected can be categorised into fall by determine whether every detected joints is in the fall motion.
